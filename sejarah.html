Sejarah Universitas AKI

Universitas AKI, disingkat UNAKI, merupakan penggabungan dari STIMIK AKI, STIBA AKI, APIKES AKI, dan STIE AKI, dengan Surat Keputusan Menteri Pendidikan Nasional Nomor 161/D/O/2003.

1994 - STIMIK AKI

In 1994, the Bina Anak Indonesia Foundation established a College in the field of computers known as STIMIK AKI in accordance with the Decree of the Minister of Education and Culture of the Republic of Indonesia NO. 09/D/O/95 so that it is able to provide the workforce needed by the market by opening various Study Programs in the field of computer for both academic and professional paths.

Gambar 1: Sejarah STIMIK AKI

1997 - STIBA AKI

To meet the need for human resources in the field of foreign languages, 3 (three) years later, precisely on November 5, 1997, a Foreign Language College was established, known as STIBA AKI in accordance with the Decree of the Minister of Education and Culture of the Republic of Indonesia No. 74/D/O/97.

Gambar 2: Sejarah STIBA AKI

1998 - APIKES AKI

Considering that the available resources were considered quite adequate, in 1998 the Academy of Health Recording and Informatics was established, known as APIKES AKI in accordance with the Decree of the Minister of Health of the Republic of Indonesia No. HK.00.06.1.1.296.5.

Gambar 3: Sejarah APIKES AKI

1999 - STIE AKI

Then, in order to meet the needs of human resources needed by the community in the economic sector, in 1999 the College of Economics was established, known as STIE AKI in accordance with the Decree of the Minister of Education and Culture of the Republic of Indonesia No. 74/D/O/99 dated April 7, 1999.

Gambar 4: Sejarah STIE AKI

2003 - Universitas AKI

For efficiency and effectiveness in several fields and towards global competition, the four institutions within AKI were merged into Universitas AKI (Abadi Karya Indonesia) abbreviated as UNAKI under the auspices of the AKI University Foundation in accordance with the Decree of the Minister of National Education of the Republic of Indonesia No. 161/D/O/2003.

Gambar 5: Sejarah Universitas AKI
